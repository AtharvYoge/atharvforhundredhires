## Key insights from this video
- To keep a team motivated and aligned, it is essential to have visible, real-time metrics, often referred to as a "scoreboard". Chandler explains that just as sports rely on scores to make the game fun and competitive, businesses need clear KPIs—like "green days" versus "red days" so every team member knows whether they are winning or losing.
- Beyond the common obsession with customer acquisition, there are only three fundamental ways to grow a business: getting more customers, increasing your average order value (AOV), or increasing the lifetime value (LTV) of those customers. Chandler emphasizes that often the most impactful growth comes from optimizing AOV or LTV rather than purely focusing on new, expensive marketing channels.
- When attracting top-tier talent, it is more effective to use "reverse selling". Instead of trying to convince a candidate that your company is perfect, you should provide enough transparent content about your culture and challenges such as their own company documentary that the right candidate convinces themselves the role is the perfect fit for their career trajectory.
