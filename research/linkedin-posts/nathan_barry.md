Should you follow your passion?

Chase Reeves says no. Follow your curiosity instead.

Passion points backward. It's what you used to love and what people remind you of.

Curiosity is present tense. It's what's alive for you right now.

Your interest in things will fade sometimes. That's fine.

Stick with something when you believe it's taking you somewhere and you want to see where that leads.

What are you curious about right now?
