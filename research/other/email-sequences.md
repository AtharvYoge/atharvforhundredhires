# Webinar Email Sequences

Synthesized from studying Amy Porterfield, Jason Fladlien 
and Joel Erway's documented email strategies.

---

## Sequence 1: Pre Webinar (Registration to Show-Up)

### Email 1 — Send immediately after registration
Subject: You're in! Here's what to expect on [Day], [Date]
Goal: Confirm registration, build anticipation, get calendar add

Hey [First Name],

You just secured your spot for [Webinar Title].

Here is what is happening on [Date] at [Time]:

I am going to show you [specific promise 1], [specific promise 2], 
and [specific promise 3] — in 60 minutes or less.

Click here to add it to your calendar: [CALENDAR LINK]

One thing: show up live. I am sharing [specific bonus] that 
I am NOT putting in the replay.

See you [Day],
[Name]

---

### Email 2 — Send 48 hours before
Subject: [First Name], your webinar is in 2 days
Goal: Re-confirm, remind of the outcome, kill reasons to skip

Hey [First Name],

Quick reminder: [Webinar Title] is happening [Day] at [Time].

If you only take one thing away from this training, it will be 
[single most powerful insight].

Here is what one attendee said last time:
"[Short testimonial about a specific result]"

Save your seat: [JOIN LINK]

---

### Email 3 — Send morning of the webinar
Subject: Today's the day — join us at [Time]
Goal: Maximize show-up rate

[First Name] — we are going live TODAY at [Time].

The single biggest mistake I see [target audience] make is [mistake].

Today I am going to show you exactly how to fix it.

Join link (save this): [LINK]

See you in a few hours.

---

### Email 4 — Send 15 minutes before
Subject: Starting in 15 minutes — join now
Goal: Last push for show-ups

We go live in 15 minutes.

Click here to join: [LINK]

I will be starting right on time. Do not miss the first 10 minutes — 
that is where I share [teaser of best content].

## Sequence 2: No-Show Follow-Up

### Email 1 — Send 1 hour after webinar ends
Subject: You missed it — here's what happened
Goal: Create FOMO, offer replay

Hey [First Name],

We missed you today.

Here is what you missed:
- [specific thing 1]
- [specific thing 2]  
- [specific thing 3]

The good news: I have a replay available for the next 48 hours.

Watch it here: [REPLAY LINK]

Fair warning — I am taking it down [specific date/time].

---

### Email 2 — Send 24 hours after
Subject: Replay comes down in 24 hours
Goal: Drive replay views before deadline

[First Name], the replay comes down tomorrow at midnight.

If you have not watched it yet, this is your last chance.

[REPLAY LINK]

After that, the only way to get this content is to [next step].

---

## Sequence 3: Post-Webinar (Attendees Who Did Not Buy)

### Email 1 — Send 1 hour after webinar
Subject: Thanks for joining — here's your next step
Goal: Warm follow-up while they are still engaged

Hey [First Name],

Thanks for joining [Webinar Title] today.

If you are ready to [main transformation], here is how to get started:

[OFFER LINK]

If you have questions before deciding, just reply to this email. 
I read every reply.

---

### Email 2 — Send 24 hours after
Subject: A question for you, [First Name]
Goal: Personal, address objections directly

Hey [First Name],

Quick question: what is holding you back?

Most people I talk to say it is one of three things:
1. "I am not sure it will work for me specifically"
2. "The timing is not right"
3. "I need to think about it"

If it is #1 — here is a story from [customer name] who had the 
same concern: [2 sentence story with result]

If it is #2 — I get it. But [reason why waiting costs them].

If it is #3 — I am happy to hop on a 10-minute call.
Book here: [CALENDAR LINK]

---

### Email 3 — Send 48 hours after
Subject: Closing [offer name] tonight at midnight
Goal: Hard deadline, final push

[First Name], this is the last email I will send about [offer name].

Tonight at midnight, [price goes up / doors close / bonus disappears].

If you want to [main outcome], now is the time.

[OFFER LINK]

After midnight, I will not be able to offer [specific bonus or price].
