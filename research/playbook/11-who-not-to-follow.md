## Who I Would Not Recommend Following and Why

---

## Alex Berman

Alex Berman is a legitimate cold outreach practitioner and his content
on B2B email sequences is genuinely useful for getting people into a
webinar. However, his YouTube and LinkedIn content has shifted toward
personal branding and agency building topics rather than practical
webinar or funnel strategy. His most watched content in 2024 is about
growing a YouTube channel and building a personal brand not webinar funnels.

For this specific topic — webinar funnels from zero — his content is thin.
He covers the top of funnel step (cold outreach to webinar invite) well,
but has almost nothing on webinar structure, show-up optimisation, or
post webinar conversion. Following him as a primary source for webinar
funnel strategy gives you an incomplete picture.

He is a strong source for cold outreach.
He is a weak source for the full webinar funnel.

I included him because the cold outreach to webinar invite angle is
underrepresented in the other sources and is genuinely useful for teams
with no existing audience.

Better alternative: Joel Erway covers the full funnel — outreach,
webinar structure, and post-webinar follow-up — with more depth and
more B2B SaaS specificity than Berman.
