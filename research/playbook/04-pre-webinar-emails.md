# Phase 4: Pre-Webinar Email Sequence

Send exactly four emails between registration and the live event.

---

## Email 1 — Immediately after registration

Subject: You are in — here is what to expect on [date]

Confirm their spot. Tell them exactly what they will walk away with.
Ask them to add the event to their calendar.
Mention one thing you will share live that will NOT be in the replay.

---

## Email 2 — 48 hours before

Subject: [First Name], your webinar is in 2 days

Remind them of the outcome. Include one short testimonial or result
from someone you have helped with this topic before.

---

## Email 3 — Morning of the webinar

Subject: Today is the day — we go live at [time]

Short. Four sentences maximum. Reinforce the one biggest thing
they will learn. Include the join link.

---

## Email 4 — 15 minutes before

Subject: Starting in 15 minutes

Just the join link and one line of urgency.
