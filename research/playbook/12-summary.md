## Summary: The Minimum Viable Webinar Funnel

Here is a brief for this playbook/SOP, do these five things in order:

1. Pick one painful problem your ideal customer has right now

2. Write a title using:
   "How to [outcome] in [timeframe] even if [objection]"

3. Find one partner who already has your audience — offer to run a free
   educational webinar for their community
   (the Nathan Barry / ConvertKit playbook)

4. Send four reminder emails between registration and the live session

5. End the webinar with one clear next step — a free trial, a call
   booking or a direct purchase link

Everything else in this playbook is optimisation.
These five steps are the foundation.
