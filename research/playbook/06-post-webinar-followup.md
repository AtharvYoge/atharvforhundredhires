## Phase 6: Post Webinar Follow Up

---

## For people who attended but did not buy — send three emails

Email 1 — 1 hour after webinar
Subject: Thanks for joining — here is your next step
Goal: Warm follow-up while they are still engaged. Thank them, restate
the offer, make it easy to reply with questions.

Email 2 — 24 hours after
Subject: A question for you, [First Name]
Goal: Address objections directly. List the three most common reasons
people hesitate and answer each one honestly.

Email 3 — 48 hours after
Subject: Closing [offer name] tonight at midnight
Goal: Hard deadline. Offer closes. One clear link. No exceptions.

---

## For people who registered but did not show up — send two emails

Email 1 — 1 hour after webinar ends
Subject: You missed it — here is what happened
Goal: Create FOMO. List three specific things covered. Offer replay
for 48 hours only. Give a specific takedown time.

Email 2 — 24 hours after
Subject: Replay comes down in 24 hours
Goal: Last chance urgency. One link. Short email.
