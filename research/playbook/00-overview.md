## Webinar Funnel from Zero — Playbook Overview

**Topic:** Webinar Funnel from Zero (B2B SaaS)

**Author:** Atharv Yoge

**Based on research from:** Russell Brunson, Amy Porterfield, Jason Fladlien,
Joel Erway, Todd Herman, Rand Fishkin, Alex Berman, Wes Bush,
Katelyn Bourgoin, Nathan Barry

**Full sources:** /research/sources.md

---

## What this playbook is

A step-by-step system for building a webinar funnel from zero for a B2B SaaS
company from choosing a topic to collecting registrations, running the live
session and converting attendees into paying customers.

Every recommendation cites the specific source it came from.
