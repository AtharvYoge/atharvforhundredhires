## Phase 2: Build Your Registration Page

---

## Step 2.1 — Your registration page needs exactly five elements

1. A headline using the formula from 01-choose-topic.md Step 1.2
2. Three bullet points: what they will learn, stated as outcomes not topics
3. A photo or short video of you (builds trust before the webinar)
4. The date, time, and duration (be specific — "60 minutes" not "about an hour")
5. One registration form field only: email address

Do not add testimonials, long bios, or multiple calls to action.
Every extra element reduces conversions.

Source: Amy Porterfield, Online Marketing Made Easy framework,
https://youtu.be/HZSBiOvxNNQ?si=YNgIb_clXEvxpc4v — November 2025

## Step 2.2 — Target a 30–40% registration-to-show-up rate

Industry average show-up rate is 15–25%.
If you hit 30–40%, your email sequence is working.
If you are below 20%, your reminder emails are the problem, not your topic.

Source: Joel Erway, micro-webinar benchmarks,
