## Phase 1: Choose Your Webinar Topic

---

## Step 1.1 — Pick one specific painful problem, not a broad theme

Do not run a webinar called "How to grow your SaaS." Run one called
"How to reduce churn in your first 90 days before customers forget why
they signed up."

The more specific the problem, the higher the registration rate.

## Step 1.2 — The webinar title is the most important thing you will write

The title is your ad. It runs before anyone sees your slides, your face,
or your offer. A bad title kills registration before the funnel even starts.

Use this formula:
How to [specific outcome] in [timeframe] even if [biggest objection]

Examples:
- "How to onboard your first 50 SaaS customers in 30 days even if you
  have no marketing team"
- "How to reduce churn by 40% in one quarter even if your product is
  still early"

Source: Russell Brunson, "Perfect Webinar Secrets" framework

https://www.clickfunnels.com/blog/complete-guide-high-converting-webinar/ — published January 2016 (updated March 2026)

## Step 1.3 — Validate the topic before building anything

Before you build slides, post this on LinkedIn:

"I am thinking of running a free training on [topic]. Would you attend?
What is your biggest question about this?"

If fewer than 20 people respond positively, change the topic.
If people start asking specific questions in the comments, those questions
become your webinar outline.

Source: Rand Fishkin, SparkToro webinar validation process
